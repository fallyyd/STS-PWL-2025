Me Before You

Jojo Moyes
★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

"Me Before You" is a contemporary romance novel by Jojo Moyes. It follows Louisa Clark, a young woman who becomes the caregiver for Will Traynor, a once-adventurous man left paralyzed after an accident, leading to an unexpected and life-changing love story.

PUBLISHER: Penguin Books

FIRST PUBLISH: January 5, 2012

LANGUAGE: English

PAGES: 369

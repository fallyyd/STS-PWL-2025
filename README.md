# PustakaGo
An online library platform where users can borrow books to read anytime, anywhere. Our goal is to make reading more accessible by connecting readers with a digital collection of books.

## Features

-Browse a wide collection of books across genres.
-Borrow books online and read them from your account.
-Search and filter books by title, author, or category.
-Track borrowed books and return them with ease.
-User-friendly interface with responsive design.

## Usage 

-Create an account or log in.
-Browse the available books.
-Click Borrow to add a book to your borrowed list.
-Access your borrowed books in the My Library section.
